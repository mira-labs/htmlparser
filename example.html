Car insurance groups explained

  1. 1Independent testing company Thatcham puts new cars through their paces
  2. 2Impact performance, damage costs, repairability, theft prevention and other factors are tested
  3. 3A group number from 1-50 is assigned, which car insurance companies use to help set the price of your quote. 1 is cheaper, 50 is pricey!
